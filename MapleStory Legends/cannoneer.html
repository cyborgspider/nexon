Cannoneer Mercedes Demon Slayer
Event

A young beginner with dreams of becoming an Explorer sets a course for Maple Island. After being attacked by Balrog and swept away to the tropical Coco Island, a new region near the Nautilus, this young pirate and his sidekick monkey adapt a ship's weapons into a one-of-a-kind Hand Cannons to battle against evil!

Class Audition

The Cannoneer's skills focus on area-of-effect damage at long range.

Cannon Bazooka

Overload your cannon and fire a powerful shot that attacks multiple enemies.

Monkey Madness

Detonate a large amount of special bombs simultaneously to attack and stun several monsters at once.

Nautilus Strike

Command the Battleship Nautilus to bombard monsters on the map!
